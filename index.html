ClassFlow packs native Android optimization, hardware shaders, and calendar sync into a premium product.

Unified Week Layout

Features perfect Saturday grid mapping with clean overflow layouts, ensuring weekend lectures remain visible and aligned.

Wallpaper-Adaptive Widgets

Scans system wallpaper color palettes programmatically to swap text colors (deep dark vs neon white) maintaining readability.
